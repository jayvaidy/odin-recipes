Tator Tot Casserole

Description

Ingredients

  • Hamburger
  • Mushroom Soup
  • Cheddar Cheese
  • Tator-Tots

Steps

  1. Brown hamburger and stir in mushroom soup
  2. Pour into casserole dish
  3. Line tator tots on top of Hamburger
  4. Bake until tator tots are brown
  5. Sprinkle with cheddar cheese
  6. Put in oven at 350 until cheese melts
