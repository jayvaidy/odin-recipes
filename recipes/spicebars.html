Spice Bars

Description

Ingredients

  • Shortening
  • Sugar
  • Brown Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla
  • Unsifted Flour
  • Baking Soda
  • Salt
  • Cinnamon
  • Ground Cloves
  • Nutmeg
  • Semi-Sweet Chocolate Chips

Steps

  1. Mix together
  2. Spread on ungreased pans
  3. Bake at 375 degrees for 20 minutes
  4. Cut and let cool
