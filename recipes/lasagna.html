Lasagna

Description

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato Paste
  • Crushed Tomatoes
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Sugar
  • Basil Leaves
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Parmesan
  • Ricotta
  • Mozzarella
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Make meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
